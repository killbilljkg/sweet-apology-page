

# Love/Apology Page for Apsara 💙🌷

## Design
- **Theme**: Blue tulips & white — soft white background, blue (#3B82F6 / sky blue) accents, tulip decorative elements via emoji/CSS
- **Typography**: Elegant, romantic feel with soft rounded fonts
- **Layout**: Single scrolling page with smooth sections, gentle fade-in animations

## Sections (top to bottom)

1. **Hero** — "Hey Apsara ❤️" with subtitle "I made this little page just for you."
2. **Main Message** — The heartfelt apology text about caring and not meaning to ignore
3. **Short & Sweet** — "You're special to me. More than you probably realize."
4. **3 Reasons I Like You** — Heart-accented list with cards
5. **Promise Section** — "A small promise" about communicating better
6. **Our Little Moments** — Photo grid with 6 placeholder image slots (you can upload photos after)
7. **Ending** — "Apsara… can we be okay now?" with emotional closing text
8. **Forgive Me Button** — "Click if you forgive me 🥺❤️" — sends a server-side API call (records that she clicked) and shows a cute animation/thank-you message
9. **Footer** — "Made with love by Josekutty ❤️"

## Interactions
- Cute heart animation on button click
- Button triggers a server function (can be wired to any webhook/notification later)
- Shows a sweet confirmation message after clicking
- Gentle scroll animations for each section
